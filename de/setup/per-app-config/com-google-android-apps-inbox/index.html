Inbox by Gmail

Oversec can not directly enter text into the body of an email. The body of an email can be encrypted via the compose button

Invisible encoding is not supported in emails, please use Base64 or Ascii-Armored PGP instead.

On Android 4.x, it is not possible to display/decrypt encrypted mails. However, you can still use the GMail app to compose and send encrypted mails.
